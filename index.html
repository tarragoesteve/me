About me

I was born in Barcelona in 1995. There I studied a double degree program in Informatics Engineering & Industrial Engineering at UPC (CFIS). I mostly specialize in deep learning and I'm looking forward to applying reinforcement learning algorithms to the control of autonomous robots.

In my free time I practice soccer and tennis weekly. I am a great skier and scuba diver. Apart from making sports I do also love playing strategy board games as Catan, Risk or MTG. I don't like video games, but I make an exception with Pokemon which I am a huge fan.

Experience
Since 2018 I have been working on my Bachelor Thesis at MIT (AutoID Lab) under the supervision of Prof. Brian Subirana.
Researched on computational finances by creating interactive educational material and clustering financial data with R Shiny.
Designed a tool to automatically detect errors on servos and an algorithm to detect mediaedge using the cutter signal.

Topic Responsible of an European Engineering Competition coordinating a team of 10 members in order to design 3 tasks that 100 students had to solve in a limited time.

Developed the back-end of a polling web application so our members can vote. It's running on Node JS, MongoDB and has a Google credentials.

I have attended 7 hackathons in different cities including Cambridge, Stirling and Barcelona.
