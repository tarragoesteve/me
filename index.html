About me

I was born in Barcelona in 1995. There I studied a double degree program in Informatics Engineering & Industrial Engineering at UPC (CFIS). Since 2018 I have been working on my Bachelor Thesis at MIT (AutoID Lab) under the supervision of Prof. Brian Subirana. I mostly specialize in deep learning and I'm looking forward to applying the reinforcement learning algorithm to the control of autonomous robots.

In my free time I practice soccer and tennis weekly. I am a great skier and scuba diver. Apart from making sports I do also love playing strategy board games as Catan, Risk or MTG. I don't like video games, but I make an exception with Pokemon which I am a huge fan.
